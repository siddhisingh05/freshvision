About FreshVision

An end-to-end AI system for automated food freshness detection.

Why we built this

Manual food freshness inspection is inconsistent and unscalable. Consumers and supply chain operators lack an accessible, accurate tool to assess food quality from a simple image.

FreshVision solves this with a two-stage deep learning pipeline — YOLOv8 for food detection and EfficientNetB0 for classification — achieving 92% accuracy on the test set.
