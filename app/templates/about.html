About FreshVision

ML Mini Project · GLA University · B.Tech CSE (AI/ML) Sem 4 · 2025-26

Project Overview

FreshVision is a unified AI-based food freshness classification system that uses a two-stage deep learning pipeline — YOLOv8 for food detection and EfficientNetB0 for freshness classification — to assess whether food is Fresh, Semi-Fresh, or Rotten from a single uploaded image.
